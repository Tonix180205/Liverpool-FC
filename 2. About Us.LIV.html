Liverpool FC is one of the most successful football clubs in England and Europe. Founded in 1892 , the club has played its home games at Anfield since its inception. Liverpool is known for its passionate fan base, iconic anthem " You'll Never Walk Alone ," and intense rivalries, particularly with Manchester United and Everton .

ACHIVEMENTS

  • - Domestic Titles : Liverpool has won 20 English league titles , including their latest triumph in 2025 under Arne Slot ⁽¹⁾.
  • - European Success : The club has won six UEFA Champions League titles , making them the most successful English team in European competition⁽¹⁾.
  • - Other Major Trophies : Liverpool has secured eight FA Cups , ten League Cups , and four UEFA Super Cups ⁽¹⁾.

CLUB IDENTITY

  • - Nickname : "The Reds"
  • - Stadium : Anfield (Capacity: 61,276 )
  • - Owner : Fenway Sports Group
  • - Current Head Coach : Arne Slot

Liverpool FC has a rich history of legendary players, including Steven Gerrard, Kenny Dalglish, Ian Rush, Mohamed Salah , and many more. The club continues to be a dominant force in English and European football.
