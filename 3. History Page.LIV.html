Liverpool FC was founded in 1892 after a dispute between Everton FC and their club president, John Houlding , over the rental of Anfield stadium. Houlding decided to create a new club, and Liverpool FC was born.

Early Success (1892–1959)

  • - Liverpool joined the Football League in 1893 .
  • - Won their first league title in 1901 .
  • - Continued success in the 1920s and 1940s , winning multiple league titles.
  • - Relegated to the Second Division in 1954 , leading to a period of struggle.

The Shankly Era (1959–1985)

  • - Bill Shankly became manager in 1959 and transformed the club.
  • - Liverpool won their first FA Cup in 1965 .
  • - Under Bob Paisley , Liverpool dominated Europe, winning six league titles and three European Cups .
  • - The Heysel Stadium disaster in 1985 led to English clubs being banned from European competitions.

Premier League Era & Modern Success (1985–Present)

  • - Liverpool struggled in the 1990s , failing to win the league.
  • - Won the UEFA Champions League in 2005 , famously coming back against AC Milan.
  • - Under Jürgen Klopp , Liverpool won the Premier League in 2020 , their first league title in 30 years.
  • - In 2025 , under Arne Slot , Liverpool secured their 20th league title ⁽¹⁾.

Liverpool remains one of the most successful and widely supported clubs in world football.
